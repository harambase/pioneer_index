About me

My name is 傅天煜, and you can call me Flora. I'm a middle school girl who loves electronic music and stick-figures. I'm fourteen years old now, and I spend a lot of my spare time watching duels between my favourite stick-figures and reading their story by fans. Sometimes I watch some Sci-Fics amd action movies.

>

    Something about my life

  • Now I am in Chengdu Pioneer School, an innovation school. I've been there for a term. Actually I was born in Shenzhen, a warm coastal city which is one of the most prosperous cities in China. I love playing with water and sand on the beach, but I prefer a cooler city rather than Shenzhen. Ironically, I can't swim at all! XD
  • There are so many tasty dim sums in Shenzhen: steamed fish, barbecue pork buns, steamed creamy custard buns, fried pigeons, osmanthus cakes etc. I miss them so much! There is fantastic spicy food in Chengdu, but I can't eat too much.
  • I am a new rhg fan. And I go to hyun's dojo to watch the animations about stick-figures(or seriously, rock hard gladiators). Hyun is the founder of this website, and his character is Yoyo (the dark blue stick-figure who uses a nuclear yoyo as his weapon), and I also like him very much.
